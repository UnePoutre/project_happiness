About Project Happiness

Welcome to Project Happiness, where we believe that happiness grows when shared. Our "HappyJar" is a digital collection of joyful moments from people all around the world.

Every day, we invite you to share one happy moment from your day - no matter how small. These moments are then shared with others who might need a little boost of happiness.

It's simple: write your happy thought, add it to our HappyJar, and brighten someone's day. Or read through the jar when you need a reminder of all the good in the world.
